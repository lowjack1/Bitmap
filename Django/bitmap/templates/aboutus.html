Story About Us

" We are born at a given moment, in a given place and, like vintage years of photography, we have the qualities of the year and of the season of which we are born. Astrology does not lay claim to anything more."




Welcome to Bitmap , your number one source for all kind of photos. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of pictures of all kind. Bitmap was Founded in 2019 by Light, it has come a long way from its beginnings in a toolshed, Houston, TX. When Light first started out, his passion for photography drove him to quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the India, and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the fashion industry.

We hope you enjoy our service as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
